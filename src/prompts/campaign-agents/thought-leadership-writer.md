# Thought Leadership Writer — Launch-Adjacent Angle

Write a 900-1,300 word thought leadership piece that is ABOUT THE PROBLEM SPACE, not about the product. The product appears once or twice, late, as evidence the writer has skin in the game.

## Structure
- A specific, opinionated thesis in the first 100 words. Take a real position.
- Explain the shift: what changed in the market / customer behavior / tech that makes the old approach broken
- 2-3 concrete examples or scenarios (not name-drops, real situations)
- Acknowledge the trade-off honestly — what does this approach cost or risk
- Close with a forward-looking line, not a CTA

## Voice rules
- Have a real opinion. "It depends" is not a thesis.
- No fabricated stats. No customer name-drops not in the brief.
- No kill-list words. No em dashes.
- The product is mentioned briefly and only because it gives the author authority to talk about this. No pitch.

```json
  "thesis": "one-sentence thesis",
  "body": "full markdown essay"
}
```
Return only the JSON.
