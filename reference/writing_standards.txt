ESSAY DEPTH (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
- Write essays that happen to be optimized, not SEO listicles dressed up with personality
- At least 2-3 sections per article must be 400+ words of sustained prose argument (deep dives)
- Target ratio: 60% flowing prose, 25% bullet/numbered lists, 15% short punchy sections
- A section should NOT be: H2 → intro sentence → bullet list → done
- A section SHOULD be: multiple paragraphs developing the argument → maybe a list for specifics → more prose on what it means
- Vary section lengths dramatically: some 100 words, some 500+ words
- Every section must connect to the one before and after it with transitional phrases
- The article should read as one continuous argument, not 8 independent mini-articles

PARAGRAPH STRUCTURE
- Paragraphs developing an argument should be 3-5 sentences
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences) for emphasis, pivot moments, key claims only
- Not every paragraph should be the same length — vary aggressively
- Stay with an idea long enough to develop it before moving to the next point

SEO STRUCTURE
- H2 headings mandatory, containing semantic keyword relevance
- Primary keyword in H1 title, first 100 words, at least 2 H2s, and meta description
- Secondary keywords woven naturally into H2s and body text
- H3s for long sections covering distinct sub-points
- First paragraph under each H2 directly answers the question implied by the heading (AEO)
- FAQ section required: 5-7 questions as natural search queries, answers 2-4 self-contained sentences
- No colons in headings
- No em dashes anywhere in the article

VOICE & STYLE
- No passive voice
- No "it's not X, it's Y" constructions
- No filler openers: "In today's world", "In the ever-evolving landscape", "It's important to note"
- Never two consecutive sentences starting with the same word
- No AI sign-off phrases: "In conclusion, it's clear that"
- Sound like someone explaining their actual process, not compiling Google results
- Acknowledge complexity honestly
- Include specific operational details: time estimates, tool names, team configurations

FORMATTING VARIETY
- Never use the same formatting type for more than 2 consecutive sections
- Bullet lists when: presenting 3+ parallel items, comparing options, listing features
- Numbered lists when: presenting sequential steps, ranked items, or a process
- Prose paragraphs when: explaining concepts, making arguments, telling a story, doing walkthroughs
- Never convert a section to bullets just to break up text
- Minimum 3 items in any list
- Lists are seasoning, not the main course

CITATIONS & LINKS
- Minimum 3 verified external citations per article, linked to authoritative sources
- Every external statistic must have a markdown link
- Every internal link must use 2-4 word natural anchor text
- Cut "According to a recent study by..." preambles — cite inline

ANCHOR TEXT RULES
- 2-4 words maximum — never a full sentence
- Good: [content-led growth], [B2B conversion rates], [AI writing tools]
- Bad: [According to a recent study by HubSpot that found], [click here to read more about]
- The link should feel invisible in the sentence

META & TECHNICAL
- Meta title: 55-60 characters, primary keyword within first 5 words
- Meta description: 150-160 characters, starts with an action verb, includes primary keyword
- Slug: clean, keyword-rich, under 60 characters
- Word count: 2,000-3,000 standard posts, 3,000-5,000 pillar content
